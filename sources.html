Sources providing help:

Hotlines and Helplines:

1. National Eating Disorders Association (NEDA) Helpline (USA):
- Phone: 1-800-931-2237
- Text: Text "NEDA" to 741741
- Website: NEDA Helpline

Description: Provides support, resources, and treatment options for individuals affected by eating disorders and body image issues.

2. Kids Helpline (Australia):
- Phone: 1800 55 1800
- Website:Kids Helpline

Description:Offers free, confidential counseling services for young people aged 5-25, including support for body image issues.

3. The Butterfly Foundation (Australia):
- Phone: 1800 33 4673
- Website:The Butterfly Foundation

Description:Provides support and resources for individuals experiencing eating disorders and body image issues.

    Specific steps to enhance body image:

  1. Explore your personal body image with its strengths and limitations.
  2. Confront thinking distortions related to your body.
  3. Challenge misleading assumptions about body appearance.
  4. Accept and love who you are.
  5. Be comfortable with your body.
  6. Have positive experiences with your body.
  7. Be a friend to your body with positive affirmations.
